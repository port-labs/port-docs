:::tip Existing secrets
If you have already installed Port's {props.integration} integration, these secrets should already exist in your portal.  
To view your existing secrets:
1. Click on the `...` button in the top right corner of your Port application.
2. Choose **Credentials**, then click on the `Secrets` tab.
:::
